Frequently Asked Questions

What is EmuDock? EmuDock is a modern launcher and library manager designed to organize, launch, and manage your emulators and games in one unified interface. Our goal is to make retro and modern gaming accessible, streamlined, and enjoyable for everyone.
Is EmuDock free to use? Absolutely. EmuDock is completely free and open source. You are welcome to use, modify, and contribute to the project. We believe in open access for all gamers.
How do I get started with EmuDock? Simply download EmuDock from our Download page, install it on your system, and add your favorite emulators and games. Our intuitive interface will guide you through the setup process so you can start playing right away.
Which platforms and emulators are supported? EmuDock is compatible with any emulator that runs on your operating system. You can add and manage emulators for a wide range of platforms, including classic consoles and handhelds, as well as modern systems.
Where can I find support or ask questions? For help, updates, or to connect with the community, please visit our Discord server or GitHub repository. Our team and community members are always happy to assist you.
