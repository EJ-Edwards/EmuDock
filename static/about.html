About EmuDock

EmuDock is a free, open source launcher and library for your emulators and games.
Our goal is to make retro gaming easy, fun, and accessible for everyone.

Whether you're a casual player or a hardcore collector, EmuDock helps you organize, launch, and enjoy your favorite titles from a single command center.
